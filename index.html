Frequently asked questions

How is this different from just using ChatGPT?

ChatGPT can't see your data. Arete AI™ connects directly to your wearables and training logs, then analyzes your actual sleep, HRV, and workout history. When you ask "should I train today?", you get an answer based on YOUR recovery state, not generic advice.

What if the system keeps reducing my workouts?

It doesn't default to easy. When you're ready to push, it tells you to push harder. Last month, 23% of adjustments INCREASED intensity because users were under-training relative to their recovery. You can also override any adjustment.

What if I only have an Apple Watch?

That's plenty. Apple Watch tracks sleep, heart rate, and workouts. The system works with one device. More devices = richer insights, but one is enough to start.

Is this iOS only?

Yes, currently iOS only. Android is on our roadmap. Sign up for the beta and we'll notify you when Android launches.

Is my health data private?

Yes. Your data is encrypted, never sold, and never used to train AI models. You can export or delete everything at any time. We make money from subscriptions, not your data.

What if Arete AI™ gives me bad advice?

Arete AI™ provides insights based on your data, not medical advice. It's designed to help you understand patterns and make informed decisions. For injuries or medical concerns, always consult a healthcare professional.
